How to make Grilled Cheese In A Microwave

So hopefully you know what all of these things are...

Ingredients

- Any bread of your choice

- Any cheese of your choice

- 1x Microwave

- 1x Butterknife (Optional)

- 2x Hands

- 1x Plate

Let's begin

1. Place your x1 cheese on a piece of bread. Feel free to add another piece of bread on top of your cheese.

2. With your hand(s) lift the plate you put your bread and cheese on and insert it into the microwave

3. Set the timer on the microwave for 30-60 seconds depending on how much cheese you want to melt into the bread.

4. Once your microwave has beeped, your food is ready

Just in case you can't use a microwave or do not know how, this tutorial section is for you then!

1. Locate a microwave to use for reheating or cooking

2. Open the microwave pressing down on the rectangular button looking a lot like a spacebar

3. Insert whatever food you want reheated or cooked and close the microwave

4. Choose how long you want it to cook for, just not too long or it will melt

5. When ready, the microwave will make a beeping sound and you can take your reheated/cooked food from the microwave

Enjoy!!!
